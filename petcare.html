🐱 Taking Care of Cats

Cats are soft, playful, and loving pets! Taking care of them means keeping them healthy, happy, and safe.

🛁 Grooming

Cats clean themselves, but they still need help! Brush your cat often to keep their fur soft and remove loose hair.

  • Brush long-haired cats more often
  • Check for tangles or knots
  • Keep nails trimmed with help from an adult

🍽️ Healthy Diet

Cats need healthy food to stay strong. They should eat cat food made just for them.

  • Always give fresh water
  • Feed cat food (not human food)
  • Ask an adult before changing food

❤️ Other Important Care Tips

There is a lot of work that goes into taking care of a cat!

  • Play with your cat every day
  • Keep them indoors for safety
  • Take them to the vet for checkups
  • Give them love and attention

🎥 Cats Playing and Staying Active

Playtime is very important for keeping cats healthy, active, and happy!
