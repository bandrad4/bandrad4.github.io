🐱 Types of Cats

There are many different kinds of cats! Each breed has its own look, personality, and needs. Learn more about different cat breeds at Cat Fanciers’ Association .

Persian Cat

Persian cats have long, fluffy fur and love to relax.

Famous Cat: Snowbell from Stuart Little

Siamese Cat

Siamese cats are very vocal and love attention.

Famous Cat: Si & Am from Lady and the Tramp

Sphynx Cat

Sphynx cats have almost no fur! They feel warm and love cuddles.

Fun Fact: They need baths more often than other cats.

American Shorthair

American Shorthair cats are friendly, easygoing, and great family pets.

Fun Fact: They are excellent hunters and love to play!

Ragdoll Cat

Ragdoll cats are calm and love being held. They often go limp like a “ragdoll.”

Fun Fact: They are known for being very gentle!

Maine Coon

One of the largest cat breeds. Very friendly and playful!

Fun Fact: They sometimes have extra toes!
