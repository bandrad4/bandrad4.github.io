🎉 Fun Cat Facts

Cats are full of surprises! Here are some fun facts about them.

🐱 Super Sleepers

Cats sleep up to 16 hours a day. That means they spend most of their time resting and recharging!

👃 Unique Noses

Every cat has a unique nose print—just like a human fingerprint.

🏃 Fast Sprinters

Cats can run up to 30 miles per hour for short bursts when playing or chasing.

🐾 Whisker Superpowers

Whiskers help cats measure spaces and sense their surroundings, even in the dark.

🗣️ Talking Cats

Cats use different meows to communicate with humans, but rarely use them with other cats.

❤️ Purring Power

Cats purr when they are happy, but also when they are nervous or trying to comfort themselves.
